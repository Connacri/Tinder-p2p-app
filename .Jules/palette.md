## 2025-06-28 - [Accessibility & Micro-Feedback]
**Learning:** Standardizing accessibility through ARIA labels and semantic labels is crucial for P2P/Web3-style apps that often rely heavily on icons/emojis. Adding micro-feedback like bio character counters and live toast accessibility significantly improves the "invisible" UX.
**Action:** Always verify icon-only buttons for ARIA labels and ensure that any character-limited fields have a visible counter to prevent user frustration.
